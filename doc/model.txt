Description of the model used in "Pendulum Lab"

This simulation contains models of 2 pendulums and 4 planets:
 - Moon
 - Earth
 - Jupiter
 - Planet X

Constants:
  gravity of Moon = 1.62 m/s^2
  gravity of Earth = 9.81 m/s^2
  gravity of Jupiter = 24.79 m/s^2
  gravity of Planet X = 14.2 m/s^2

Variables:
  angle - rotation angle of pendulum
  alpha - angular acceleration of pendulum
  omega - angular velocity of pendulum
  length - length of pendulum
  g - gravitational acceleration
  A - friction coefficient

Motion formulas:
  angle_1 = angle_0 + omega * dt + 0.5 * alpha_0 * dt * dt
  alpha_1 = g * length * Math.sin( angle_1 ) - A / mass^(1/3)
  omega_1 = omega_0 + 0.5 * (alpha_1 + alpha_0) * dt

Approximate period formula:
  T = 2 * Math.PI * Math.sqrt( length / g )

Energy formulas:
  potentialEnergy = mass * g * length * (1 - Math.cos( angle ))
  kineticEnergy = 0.5 * mass * (length * omega)^2
  thermalEnergy = totalEnergy - (this.kineticEnergy + this.potentialEnergy)
